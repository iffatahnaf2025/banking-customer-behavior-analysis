## Methodology Notes

### RFM Model
RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) is a proven customer segmentation framework used widely in banking and retail analytics:
- **Recency (R):** How recently did the customer transact?
- **Frequency (F):** How often do they transact?
- **Monetary (M):** How much do they spend per transaction?

Each customer receives a score on each dimension, and the combined RFM score determines their segment.
